Screen Time FAQs

What is considered excessive screen time?

Excessive screen time is generally over 2 hours daily for kids. But let’s be real, if they’re glued to the screen like a zombie, it’s time to intervene!

How does screen time affect children?

It can lead to physical issues like obesity, mental health problems, and emotional detachment. Basically, it’s a recipe for disaster!

What are healthier screen time habits?

Encourage breaks, outdoor play, and family activities. Remember, screens are not babysitters!

How can I reduce screen time?

Set limits, create tech-free zones, and lead by example. Show them that life exists beyond the screen!
